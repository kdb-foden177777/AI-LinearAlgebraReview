# Paper Result Narrative

The full benchmark contains 106 items extracted from the converted corpus. Expanded concept tagging over 80 completed API-labeled items reached micro F1=0.377 and mean Jaccard=0.324 against keyword-derived silver labels, with diagnostics showing LLM over-labeling relative to the automatic silver labels. The expanded retrieval benchmark contains 160 queries and 640 condition-query rows. Lexical retrieval reached Success@5=0.875 and MRR@5=0.751, lecture-only retrieval reached Success@5=0.650 and MRR@5=0.456, exam-only retrieval reached Success@5=0.825 and MRR@5=0.690, and concept-filtered retrieval achieved Success@5=1.000 and MRR@5=1.000 under known active concept metadata. RAG support evaluation produced supported rate=0.975 and mean support score=0.945. Agent interaction evaluation used 144 completed tasks and is reported as diagnostic counts: grading produced 47/48 concept-linked and 47/48 evidence-linked responses, hinting produced 42/48 concept-linked and 41/48 evidence-linked responses while avoiding full-solution leakage in 48/48 cases, and dialogue produced 48/48 concept-linked and 48/48 evidence-linked responses under concept-tied prompts. Overall, 137/144 responses were concept-linked and 136/144 were evidence-linked. IRT results are simulation-based and should not be described as real learner calibration.
